Our Mission

THRIVE strives to connect people facing similar medical conditions (both physical and mental) to foster a tight-knit and supportive community that supports and lifts up one another. Meet other people facing a similar medical roadblock as you through our platform and share your journey/experiences :)

Join specific medical communities

Connect with people that have the same medical illness/condition as you in individual groupchats.

Grow your knowledge

Even if you don't live a specific condition, learn more about what other people face through our information hub to extend your medical knowledge.

Provide support

Start fundraisers or help raise awareness for those living with unique conditions that need a little extra support to thrive.

Follow the lives of individuals that want to document their medical journey

THRIVE encourages our users to document and share their experiences for members to see what a day in their life is like and how they thrive
